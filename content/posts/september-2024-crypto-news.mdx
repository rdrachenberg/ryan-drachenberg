The regulatory landscape in the crypto space also saw some important developments. On **September 12**, the SEC settled a case with **eToro USA**, resulting in a $1.5 million penalty for offering unregistered crypto asset securities. As part of the agreement, eToro will limit the crypto assets available to US customers, including Bitcoin, Ether, and Bitcoin Cash [source](https://dlapiper.com). The **CFTC** also issued charges against **Uniswap Labs** for illegal digital asset derivatives trading, resulting in a $175,000 penalty.
