### üåç Global Adoption on the Rise

Global adoption of cryptocurrency has surged in 2024, with more than 560 million people now owning some form of crypto. Stablecoins, in particular, have seen rapid adoption as a cost-effective tool for cross-border payments and remittances [source](https://marketscreener.com).

As Q4 progresses, the crypto world continues to evolve, with these trends shaping the future of both traditional and decentralized finance.
