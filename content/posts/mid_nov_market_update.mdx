### Sustainable Energy Initiatives for Data Centers

<div className='mx-auto w-[300px] sm:w-[500px]'>
    <img className='rounded-lg' src='https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2022/08/26/17/27/environment-7412967_1280.jpg' />
</div>

In a groundbreaking move, Google has entered into an agreement with Kairos Power to utilize nuclear energy for powering its AI data centers. This partnership involves the development of small modular reactors (SMRs), with the first expected to be operational by 2030. This initiative reflects a broader trend among tech giants seeking sustainable energy solutions to meet the growing electricity demands of AI and cloud computing.
